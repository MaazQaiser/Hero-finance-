Bricolage Grotesque font installed.

HOW IT'S USED ON SITE
  Characters pop in (fade + spring) as each section scrolls into view, sometimes
  pointing at the call-to-action, sometimes just driving past. Car type varies by
  section for variety. On the live site this is scroll-triggered; in the PDF they're
  shown in their landed position. Poses flip horizontally to point either direction.
  Keep them as accents — one moment per section, never competing with the CTA itself.
